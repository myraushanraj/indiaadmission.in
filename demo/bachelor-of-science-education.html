Bachelor of Science Education

  • 4.50
B.Sc. cum B.Ed. is 4 years’ integrated B.Sc. and B.Ed. course. It is the one of the most sought after course in the field of teaching and education for a student who has a science background, as it helps him in saving precious 1 year of his life.

In this course you learn the art of teaching through studying various teaching subjects as well as general subjects that are taught in a B.Sc. course, apart from these, practical teaching & workshops are also done as well. You would be provided with study materials and all other necessary things during your B.Ed. degree program. The colleges that offer this course are recognized by Jiwaji University, which is a central university under UGC.

Consider pursuing B.Sc. B.Ed from a UGC recognized and NCTE affiliated college. When most of the student would have done simple graduation, you would be ready to get a professional B.Sc. B.Ed degree and would be able to apply for the post of a secondary level teacher or TGT in a particular subject specialization. Apart from having mandatory B. Ed. degree you should be CTET qualified for this Job Profile.

You would have a meaningful and peaceful life working as a TGT in a government school; the work profile would not be as hectic as employees working in other sectors. You get a timely salary, a reputed job, fixed hours, pension, insurance, medical facilities & job security if you crack the competitive exams conducted for the post of TGT by state governments.
